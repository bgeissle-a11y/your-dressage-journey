Rider Health & Wellness Log

A training journal for how your body is showing up in the saddle. Log what's changing, what's healing, what's getting in the way — so patterns can be seen.

This is a training journal, not a medical record. Log what helps you and your coach understand your riding — not clinical detail that belongs in your doctor's chart. Only log something here if it's currently affecting your riding. Baseline asymmetries live in your Physical Self-Assessment. Exercises and things you're trying live in your Rider's Toolkit.
What not to log: Please keep entries functional and riding-focused. Do not enter specific medication names or dosages, verbatim diagnoses or medical codes, mental health treatment details, or anything you'd want your doctor's office to keep private. "Right hip tight and limiting my seat" is useful. Clinical diagnoses with specific codes or test results belong in your medical chart, not here.
