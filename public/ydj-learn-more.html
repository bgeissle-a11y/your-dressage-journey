YDJ isn't an app that teaches you how to ride. It's a platform that accelerates your learning and enhances your partnership. Before the ride, after it, and in every hour in between. Everything you experience, reflected back as insight you can actually use.

The Problem

Learning between rides is a missed opportunity.

The Solution

YDJ closes the loop. Before, during, and after every ride.

⏱️Time at the barn is limited. Away from it, most riders spend unlimited energy looping or scrolling.
🧩Off-horse tools turn time away from the barn into purposeful learning.
🧠Cognitive overload keeps you in your head instead of in your body.
🎯Set intentions before you ride. Reduce cognitive load so you can feel and respond.
🐴You track one athlete. Two show up for every ride.
✨AI finds patterns across both athletes, over weeks and months, not just today.
📍Shows and events pass by without full preparation and sustained follow-through.
📅Nothing left to last minute. Every event's insights captured for next time.
🔀Patterns across rides are impossible to see from inside them.
🗺️Your Journey Map grows with you. Every pattern, milestone, and breakthrough visible over time.
One Journey. Two Athletes.

Your horse has patterns too.

YDJ is the only platform that tracks the body and mind of both horse and rider. Asymmetries, tendencies, good days and hard ones, for both of you.

Over time, the AI suggests ways to help you work better together. The platform tailors guidance to the partnership, not just the person. Because two athletes show up for every ride.

Rider

Position patterns, physical habits, mental state, learning style, off-horse practice. All tracked longitudinally and connected to what happens in the saddle.

Horse

Asymmetries, warm-up needs, tension patterns, soundness history, and how those patterns interact with yours. All surfaced from the data you're already capturing.

The Six Reflection Categories

Every dimension of your journey,
captured.

Once a week, you choose one category that resonates with where you are right now. Over time, all six give the AI a complete, multi-dimensional picture of your riding life.

"How does it look into my very soul and see the things I don't?" — Linda Klarner, pilot participant

🟦 Personal Milestone
Achievements and breakthroughs that matter to you, regardless of external recognition.
🟩 External Validation
Feedback from coaches, judges, or peers that moved you forward or shifted your perspective.
🟨 Aha Moment
The click. The realization. When theory finally connected to feeling and something made sense.
🟥 Obstacle
Challenges and frustrations. Physical, mental, financial, or partnership. The hard parts matter too.
🟧 Feel
Body awareness and physical sensation. The subtle language between horse and rider.
🟪 Connection
The relationship with your horse. Trust, understanding, partnership, and those moments of unity.
