4.4 Pre-Lesson Summary & Optional Coach Sharing

Coach sharing is entirely under your control. YDJ will never share your data with any coach unless you have explicitly enabled sharing for that specific coach in your account settings. The Pre-Lesson Summary is a rider-facing feature first; sharing it with a coach requires your affirmative action.

YDJ generates a Pre-Lesson Summary — a structured briefing drawn from your recent training data and platform outputs to help you arrive at each lesson prepared. You may optionally share it with a coach by enabling coach sharing in Settings. The following terms govern the coach sharing extension of this feature:

  • Consent is per-coach and explicit. You must individually add each coach (by name and email address) and enable sharing through Settings → Coach Sharing. No summary is ever sent to a coach without this affirmative action.
  • Consent is recorded. Your opt-in date for each coach is permanently recorded in your account as a consent audit trail and is never modified, even if you later disable sharing.
  • You may withdraw consent at any time. Turning off sharing for a coach takes effect immediately. You may also remove a coach entirely, which withdraws consent and deletes the coach record from your account.
  • Scope of sharing. The Pre-Lesson Summary shared with your coach is assembled from your existing platform data and AI-generated outputs. Your coach receives a formatted summary by email and does not receive access to your YDJ account.
  • Coach email use is limited. Your coach's email address will be used solely to deliver your Pre-Lesson Summary. YDJ will never use it for any other purpose, including marketing.
  • No coach-initiated actions. Your coach cannot log into YDJ, access your account, or modify your data.
  • Parallel truth. The Pre-Lesson Summary is drawn directly from data you have entered and outputs already generated for you. It contains no independent AI analysis and will never contradict what you see in your own account.
