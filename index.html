What is Your Dressage Journey?

A comprehensive training and reflection system that combines structured data collection with pattern analysis to help riders make their improvement systematic rather than intuitive.

  • Reflection prompts capture insights across 6 key categories
  • Post-ride debriefs track your progress systematically
  • Pattern analysis reveals connections you can't see alone
  • Evidence-based protocols emerge from your actual data
  • Personalized recommendations based on what works for you

Pilot Commitment

We're looking for riders who will actively engage with the reflection process:

  • Complete at least 5 reflection exercises
  • Complete at least 5 post-ride debriefs
  • Participate in weekly check-ins
  • Complete an exit survey at the end
  • Provide honest feedback about the experience
